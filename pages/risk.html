High Risk Investment Warning

Cryptocurrency investments are high risk. The value of your investment can go down as well as up, and you may lose some or all of the money you invest. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. Only invest money you can afford to lose entirely.

CryptoVault provides professionally managed cryptocurrency investment services involving real digital assets. Before investing, it is essential that you fully understand the risks involved. This Risk Disclaimer forms part of our Terms & Conditions and should be read in conjunction with them.

By creating an account, depositing funds, or subscribing to any investment plan, you confirm that you have read and understood this Risk Disclaimer and accept the risks described herein.

If you are uncertain about any aspect of cryptocurrency investment risk, we strongly recommend consulting an independent, qualified financial advisor before investing.

02 Market Risk

Cryptocurrency markets are among the most volatile financial markets in the world. Prices can change dramatically in very short periods of time — sometimes losing 20%, 50%, or even more of their value within days or hours.

Key market risk factors include:

  • Extreme price volatility driven by speculation, sentiment, and news events
  • Low liquidity in certain market conditions that can amplify price movements
  • Market manipulation by large holders ("whales") that can move prices significantly
  • 24/7 trading with no circuit breakers or trading halts
  • Limited historical data compared to traditional financial markets
  • Correlation with broader risk sentiment during macroeconomic events

CryptoVault's investment strategies are designed to manage these risks, but cannot eliminate them. Market conditions may cause actual returns to fall below, or significantly below, target return ranges.

04 Investment Plan Risks

4.1 No Guaranteed Returns

Target return ranges shown for each investment plan are based on historical performance of our trading strategies under various market conditions. These ranges do not constitute a guarantee, promise, or projection of future returns. Actual returns may be significantly lower, zero, or negative.

4.2 Capital Risk

You may lose some or all of the capital you invest. Unlike bank deposits, cryptocurrency investments are not protected by government deposit guarantee schemes. If our strategies perform poorly, you may receive back less than your original investment.

4.3 Liquidity Risk During Plan Period

During an active plan period, your invested capital is deployed in active positions. While we process withdrawal requests, liquidating positions in adverse market conditions may result in lower returns than expected.

Cryptocurrency markets have experienced periods of dramatic decline following periods of strong performance. A strategy that has performed well historically may perform poorly in future market cycles.

07 Regulatory & Legal Risk

The regulatory environment for cryptocurrencies is rapidly evolving and varies significantly by jurisdiction. Regulatory changes may:

  • Restrict or prohibit cryptocurrency investment services in your jurisdiction
  • Result in forced liquidation of positions or account closure
  • Impose new taxes on cryptocurrency gains
  • Require additional compliance procedures that delay transactions
  • Affect the value of cryptocurrencies themselves

It is your responsibility to ensure that using CryptoVault's services is permitted under the laws of your jurisdiction. We do not guarantee continued service availability in all regions.

08 Technology Risk

Cryptocurrency and blockchain technology carry inherent technical risks, including:

  • Smart Contract Risk: Bugs or exploits in blockchain protocols may result in loss of funds
  • Network Congestion: High blockchain network activity can delay transaction confirmation
  • Platform Downtime: Scheduled maintenance or unforeseen technical issues may temporarily restrict access
  • Cyberattacks: Despite our security measures, no platform is immune to sophisticated attacks
  • Wallet Address Errors: Transactions sent to incorrect addresses are irreversible on the blockchain
  • Fork Risk: Blockchain network upgrades ("hard forks") may affect asset value or availability

We implement robust security measures to mitigate technology risks, but cannot guarantee complete protection against all technical threats.

09 Investment Suitability

Cryptocurrency investment via CryptoVault may not be suitable for everyone. You should not invest if:

  • You cannot afford to lose the full amount you plan to invest
  • You need the funds within a short timeframe for essential expenses
  • You are not comfortable with high levels of price volatility
  • You do not understand how cryptocurrency markets work
  • You are investing based on borrowed funds or credit
  • Cryptocurrency investment is prohibited in your jurisdiction

Before investing, honestly assess your financial situation, investment experience, and risk tolerance. A good rule of thumb: only invest what you could comfortably write off completely without affecting your lifestyle.
